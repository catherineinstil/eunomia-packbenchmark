# Best in Class Packaging Tool — Project Plan
**Designer:** Cat | **Brief from:** JK | **Date:** 23 April 2026 | **Status:** Pre-sales / pitch concept

---

## Brief Summary

Eunomia (Dublin environmental consultancy) is pitching a **packaging benchmarking platform** to CAA — California's state-appointed Producer Responsible Organisation. CAA needs a tool to compare packaging efficiency across product types and set eco-modulation fees.

Eunomia want Instil to build it. Before committing, they need **concept designs for the pitch** — enough to make the tool feel real to CAA, not just a spreadsheet.

**Our job:** Produce 3–4 concept screens in Figma. Static, not a prototype. If the pitch wins, Instil builds the platform.

---

## What to Design

| # | Screen | Priority | Notes |
|---|--------|----------|-------|
| 1 | Product Type Browser | High | List/grid of packaging categories with search & filter |
| 2 | Benchmarking View | **Critical** | Best/median/worst-in-class chart across key attributes — the "money shot" |
| 3 | Data Entry / Upload | High | Touch uploading a product: image, attributes, tagging |
| 4 | Dashboard / Overview | Optional | Summary stats — only if time/scope allows |

---

## Design Direction

- **Audience:** CAA (government/regulatory) — clean, credible, professional. Not flashy.
- **Tone:** Data tool, not consumer app.
- **Visual comparables:** Airtable, regulatory reporting dashboards
- **Palette:** Neutral — no branding constraints yet (Eunomia will operate it)
- **Fidelity:** Concept level — enough to show the idea, not pixel-perfect
